Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Thank you for using eSign.

eSign is designed with privacy in mind. Your documents, signatures, and stamps are stored locally on your device and are not uploaded to our servers.

Information We Collect

Documents and Signatures

eSign allows you to import, sign, stamp, and export documents and images. All imported documents, signatures, stamps, and exported files remain on your device unless you choose to share them using your device's built-in sharing features.

We do not collect, store, or access the contents of your documents.

Analytics and Diagnostics

To improve the app and fix issues, eSign uses:

  • Firebase Analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics

These services may collect anonymous usage information, device information, app performance data, and crash reports.

In-App Purchases

eSign uses RevenueCat and Apple's App Store to manage subscriptions and purchases. RevenueCat may process anonymous purchase-related information necessary to validate purchases.

We do not collect payment card information. All payments are processed by Apple.

How We Use Information

  • Improve app performance
  • Identify and fix crashes
  • Understand feature usage
  • Improve user experience

Data Storage

Documents, signatures, stamps, and exported files are stored locally on your device. We do not provide cloud storage and do not upload your documents to our servers.

Children's Privacy

eSign is intended for a general audience and is not directed toward children under 13 years of age.

Third-Party Services

  • Apple App Store
  • RevenueCat
  • Firebase Analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be reflected by updating the date shown above.
